Margot Robbie Revives the Kitten Heel at Birds of Prey’s Premiere

Winter’s default shoe may be an insulated boot, but that hasn’t stopped stars from having fun with
footwear. Currently promoting her second turn as DC comics anti-heroine Harley Quinn in Birds of Prey,
Margot Robbie has been making the rounds in eye-catching outfits. And she outdid herself last night at the
film’s Hollywood premiere. Thanks to a flirty black empire-waist mini-dress by Khaite and
crystal-studded Alessandra Rich choker selected by stylist Kate Young, Robbie looked impossibly chic.
Yet the addition of her wrap-around slingback pumps from Attico took things to another level.

Hot pink satin with a kitten heel, Robbie’s shoes added a touch of color to her otherwise monochromatic
outfit. The unique lace-up straps and comfortable low heel made the shoes stand out, providing a final
touch of whimsy for this very memorable look.
